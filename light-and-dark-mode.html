Light And Dark Mode

What Is The Meaning Of Light And Dark Mode Feature ?

Light mode is to change the program color into white or light but dark mode is to change the program color into black or dark.

How Can I Use Light And Dark Mode Feature ?

First you must open the spellexo then you will see a moon button then click it and it will change spellexo into dark mode and if you want to change it into light mode again click on the sun button and it will change spellexo into light mode.

What Is The Important Of Light And Dark Mode Feature ?

It's very important feature because it protects your eyes from the monitor's light specifically if your monitor type is oled or amoled or super amoled.
